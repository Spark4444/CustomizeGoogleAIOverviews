Welcome to Customize Google AI Overview!
Customize Google AI Overview is a browser extension designed to enhance your experience on Fandom sites by blocking intrusive ads.
To configure your preferences, open the popup.
Pin the extension to easily access the settings.
Step 1: Click the extension puzzle icon in top-right side.
Step 2: Click the pin button to pin the extension.
Step 3: That's it now you can click on the extension icon to open the popup.
If you encounter any issues, please visit the Issues page on GitHub and leave your feedback.
Thank you for choosing Customize Google AI Overview!
